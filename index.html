About the coin

AkitaRiver - community meme coin, inspired by the famous token of the same name on the ETH. Doge's and Shiba Inu's smaller brother on the MoonRiver network.
Our task is to successfully launch AlitaRiver, further develop token and grow the community, so that early investors will be greatly rewarded with a high rate. because we value people who will believe in us and our idea from the very beginning!

Tokenomics

Our tokenomics includes several wallets where tokens will be stored, which will be used for various purposes so that our community and token rate grow rapidly.

Ecosystem

Our ecosystem so far includes only the AkitaRiver token, which has unique tokenomics and operating principles that will appeal to our entire community. For example, daily burning, lotteries and contests.

CONTESTS

We will hold various competitions for our community with prizes. This will help to increase loyalty to our project and increase the community of AkitaRiver.

BURNING

We will burn 1% of the total supply of tokens daily to maintain the market balance (we will do this while we have enough tokens from commissions).

TAX

Each transaction is charged a 10% commission in AkitaRiver tokens. After that, they get to the main wallet, from which they are distributed to other wallets allocated for improving the project.

Opportunities

We will announce all contests in our social networks, so subscribe to us and stay up to date with all the events of the project.

Lottery

Every day there will be a lottery where tokens will be raffled off. There will be 3 prizes in total, the reward for which will be used with commissions from each transaction (2%):

1) 50% of the prize pool

2) 30% of the prize pool

3) 20% of the prize pool

To participate in the lottery, you need to buy AkitaRiver for at least 0.2 MOVR (one transaction) on the day of the lottery.
